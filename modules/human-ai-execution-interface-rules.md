# Human–AI Execution Interface Rules

---

## Purpose

Ensure consistent, high-clarity interaction between human and AI by eliminating ambiguity, mixed modes, and interpretation.

---

## Core Principle

Every interaction must produce immediately usable output without clarification loops.

---

## Rules

• No placeholders in outputs  
• No mixed modes (analysis + execution combined)  
• No partial responses  
• No assumed context  
• No open-ended instructions  

---

## Interaction Model

1. Input is specific and scoped  
2. AI response is complete and structured  
3. Output is immediately usable without modification  

---

## Output Requirements

• Copy-ready when applicable  
• Clearly formatted  
• Single-mode (either analysis OR execution)  
• No hidden steps  

---

## Anti-Patterns (Disallowed)

• “You could also…” suggestions during execution  
• Responses requiring interpretation  
• Blended explanation + action  
• Incomplete outputs  

---

## Use Case

Applied when:

• prompting AI systems  
• executing workflows via AI  
• generating reusable outputs  
• maintaining clarity in complex systems  

---

## Result

• Reduced back-and-forth  
• Higher output reliability  
• Faster execution cycles  
• Consistent interaction quality
